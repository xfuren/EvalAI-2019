In addition to the terms and conditions signed by participants to access the data, following rules are applied:

  1. For the unsupervised and supervised phases of the challenge, participants can make only 1 submission per day
  2. For the final phase of the challenge, participants can make upto 5 submissions every day
  3. Evaluation metric and evaluation set can be changed at the discretion of the organizers at any time during the challenge
  4. Permissible number of submissions per day can be changed at the discretion of the organizers at any time during the challenge
